About Us

Helping Paws was founded in August 2021 with its main aim being able to rescue , aid as well as find loving and promising homes for those animals in need. Our organization has a goal to sterilize as many cats and dogs as possible in the Western Cape to ensure a better future for that animal.

Helping Paws was founded by Mikayla Beelders, a young lady with a massive passion for helping animals. Helping Paws is controlled by the idea that every single animal deserves a better chance at life and thus resulting in our organization standing up for these animals to the best of our ability.
